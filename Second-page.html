Cosmere Insights

Welcome to the second page of the Cosmere walkthrough. Here, we’ll dive deeper into the worlds and characters.

Worlds of the Cosmere

The Cosmere is full of diverse worlds, each governed by different forces and Shards. Some of the major worlds include Scadrial, Roshar, and Sel.

Characters and Their Destiny

Many characters in the Cosmere have intersecting fates, and understanding their roles across different books enriches the reading experience. Notable characters include Vin, Kaladin, and Shallan.
