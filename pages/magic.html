Understanding Cosmere Magic

Warning: This page contains minor spoilers about the magic systems in the Cosmere. If you prefer to experience the story unspoiled, consider reading the books first!

What Makes Cosmere Magic Unique?

In the Cosmere, magic isn’t just about casting spells. Each world has its own unique system of magic that follows logical rules and influences society, politics, and even the landscape. Magic in the Cosmere is connected to powerful, god-like beings called Shards, and every magic system is rooted in the fundamental laws of the universe.

Magic Systems of the Cosmere

Allomancy (Mistborn)

Allomancy is a metal-based magic system where users ingest metals and "burn" them to gain supernatural abilities. Most people can only burn one metal, but rare individuals called Mistborn can use all of them.

  • Iron and Steel: Push and pull on metal objects.
  • Pewter: Enhances strength and speed.
  • Tin: Heightens senses.
  • Brass and Zinc: Manipulate emotions.

Feruchemy (Mistborn)

Feruchemy involves storing attributes in metal objects (called metalminds) for later use.

  • Strength, Speed, and Senses: Store these attributes and use them later.
  • Memory: Store memories to recall them with perfect clarity.

Hemalurgy (Mistborn)

Hemalurgy is a dark and violent magic system that allows the transfer of abilities by driving metal spikes through a person's body. This process steals a specific trait (such as strength, intelligence, or magical abilities) and embeds it into another individual.

  • Stealing Powers: Grants Allomantic or Feruchemical abilities when a spike is used correctly.
  • Weakened Victims: The original owner of the ability is often left broken or dead.
  • Binding Connections: Hemalurgy can be used to create supernatural bonds, like control over Koloss and Steel Inquisitors.

Surgebinding (The Stormlight Archive)

Surgebinding is a system where magic users (called Surgebinders) bond with mystical spren and gain powers.

  • Gravitation: Manipulate gravity to fly.
  • Illumination: Create complex illusions.
  • Regrowth: Heal oneself or others.

BioChromatic Breath (Warbreaker)

BioChromatic Breath is a spiritual essence that every person is born with. It can be given away, and those with many Breaths gain powerful abilities.

  • Awakening: Bring inanimate objects to life.
  • Heightenings: Enhanced senses, strength, and even beauty.

AonDor (Elantris)

AonDor is a rune-based magic system where users (called Elantrians) draw complex symbols in the air to produce magical effects.

  • Light and Healing: Create light and heal wounds.
  • Teleportation: Instantly travel between locations.
