Understanding Cosmere Magic

Warning: This page contains minor spoilers about the magic systems in the Cosmere. If you want to go in completely blind, it might be best to start with the books first!

What Makes Cosmere Magic Unique?

In the Cosmere, magic isn’t just about casting spells. Each world has its own unique system of magic that follows logical rules and influences society, politics, and even the landscape. Magic in the Cosmere is connected to powerful, god-like beings called Shards, and every magic system is rooted in the fundamental laws of the universe.

Magic Systems of the Cosmere

Allomancy (Mistborn)

Allomancy is a metal-based magic system where users ingest metals and "burn" them to gain supernatural abilities. Most people can only burn one metal, but rare individuals called Mistborn can use all of them.

  • Iron and Steel: Push and pull on metal objects, creating telekinetic-like effects.
  • Pewter: Enhances strength and speed.
  • Tin: Heightens senses like sight and hearing.
  • Brass and Zinc: Manipulate emotions.

Feruchemy (Mistborn)

Feruchemy is about storing attributes in metal objects (called metalminds) that can be tapped into later. It’s a system of balance—you get back exactly what you stored, no more, no less.

  • Strength, Speed, and Senses: Store these attributes by becoming weaker temporarily, then use them later at enhanced levels.
  • Memory: Store memories to recall them with perfect clarity.

Surgebinding (The Stormlight Archive)

Surgebinding is a system where magic users (called Surgebinders) bond with mystical spren and gain powers based on fundamental forces of nature. The bond is emotional and tied to oaths or ideals that the Surgebinder must live by.

  • Gravitation: Manipulate gravity to fly or walk on walls.
  • Illumination: Create complex illusions.
  • Regrowth: Heal oneself or others.

BioChromatic Breath (Warbreaker)

BioChromatic Breath is a spiritual essence that every person is born with. It can be given away, and those with many Breaths gain powerful abilities. It’s closely tied to color, as Awakeners drain the color from objects to fuel their magic.

  • Awakening: Bring inanimate objects to life by giving them commands.
  • Heightenings: Enhanced senses, strength, and even beauty.

AonDor (Elantris)

AonDor is a rune-based magic system where users (called Elantrians) draw complex symbols in the air to produce magical effects. It’s geography-dependent, meaning its power is tied to the land and becomes weaker if you’re far from the source.

  • Light and Healing: Create light and heal wounds.
  • Teleportation: Instantly travel between locations.

Why Cosmere Magic is Unique

What makes Cosmere magic so fascinating is its logical, rule-based nature. Each system feels like it could genuinely exist because of its consistent limitations and cultural significance. As you read through the Cosmere, you’ll discover that these magic systems aren’t just about power—they shape entire societies and histories, making the universe feel alive and interconnected.
