Welcome to Seth's walkthrough of the Cosmere, SPOILER WARNING

Hello, I decided that I would make my website about something I quite knowledgeable about! A series of books that make up the universe of the Cosmere by Brandon Sanderson.

So what is the point of this website?

  • I hope to make you interested enough to check out any of the books I recommend.
  • I will talk about my favorite characters and geek out a bit about the lore.
  • I will give a timeline of the book releases and my recommended reading order.

How my journey started

  1. Mistborn: The Final Empire
  2. Elantris
  3. The way of Kings

Visit this website for offical information, books or merchandise if that's your thing!

The Cosmere is full of fascinating connections, like the relationships between Shards & their worlds or characters & their destinies. Understanding these links is key to unraveling the deeper mysteries of Brandon Sanderson's universe. So I hope we can enjoy this Journey together!
