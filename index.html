Welcome to Seth's walkthrough of the Cosmere, SPOILER WARNING

Hello! I decided to create this website to share something I'm really passionate about—the incredible series of books that make up the Cosmere universe by Brandon Sanderson.

So what is the point of this website?

  • My goal is to spark your interest in the amazing books of the Cosmere and hopefully get you excited to check out some of my recommendations.
  • I'll dive into some of my favorite characters, share insights, and geek out about the intricate lore.
  • You’ll also find a timeline of book releases and a reading order that I recommend for diving into the Cosmere.

How my journey started

  1. Mistborn: The Final Empire
  2. Elantris
  3. The Way of Kings

Feel free to visit this website for official information, books, or merchandise if that’s your thing!

The Cosmere is full of fascinating connections, like the relationships between Shards & their worlds or characters & their destinies. Understanding these links is key to unraveling the deeper mysteries of Brandon Sanderson's universe. So I hope we can enjoy this Journey together!
