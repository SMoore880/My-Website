Welcome to the Cosmere!

The Cosmere is more than just a collection of books—it's an entire universe waiting to be explored. If you're wondering where to start, this guide will help you navigate its worlds without spoilers!

What is the Cosmere?

A single spark can ignite a universe...

Imagine a collection of worlds, each with its own laws of magic, ancient legends, and warring gods. Now imagine that these worlds are not just separate stories, but pieces of a much larger mystery—one that unfolds across centuries. That’s the Cosmere. It’s not just a series of books. It’s a hidden universe, waiting to be uncovered.

Why Read the Cosmere?

Magic. Mystery. Gods who were once human. What more could you want?

The Cosmere is for readers who crave stories that don’t just entertain but challenge you to think. Each book builds upon the last, unraveling secrets and introducing you to characters who feel real—heroes, villains, and those who walk the line between. But the best part? The more you read, the more you’ll realize you’re part of something bigger.

The Cosmere Connection

Separate stories. One universe. A hidden narrative waiting to be discovered.

At first, the Cosmere might seem like a collection of unrelated books. But look closer, and you'll see the breadcrumbs Sanderson leaves behind—characters who appear where they shouldn’t, cryptic references to unseen forces, and a history far older than any one world. Every book holds a piece of the puzzle. Will you find them?
